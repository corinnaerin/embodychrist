Sunday Worship

Embody will be meeting on Sunday mornings at 10am. Kids will join us for worship then when it's time for the teaching, they'll head to their own class where they'll learn in a more kid-oriented fashion (which, of course, we hope not to get too far away from as adults). We worship in a blended style which means we take some of the thoughtful songs from today and mix them with some thoughtful hymns from the past. Art and creativity are valued at Embody and we hope to free people to worship and learn in a variety of ways.

If you have any questions, or would like more information, please feel free to contact us.

Check out the calendar or the Prosper Projects link to see what's next. Also, contact us, and we'll make sure you get connected.
