Who Are We?

Welcome to Embody. We're a new church serving and worshiping south of Holland, MI. At Embody Christ Fellowship, we do 4 things: we worship, we serve, we do life together, and we grow deeper. We do all things in a spirit of hospitality. That means, we're a place where you don't have to believe to belong! And, you don't have to have it all figured out either - everyone is at a different place on their journey with and understanding of Jesus...So, let's do this journey of life together.

Since we want to be the hands and feet of Christ, his embodiment, we want to serve the world. So, we are always looking for ways to help where help is needed. It's our goal to be part of God's work of bringing the world into wholeness and healing. So, we are always seeking the peace and prosperity of our world.

We recognize the need for stronger relationships, so that's where we focus: stronger friendships - ones that go deeper, stronger marriages, stronger parenting. We may not have it all figured out, but we'll search the answers together, because we believe God has given us all the help we need in the Bible, by his Holy Spirit, and in fellowship together.

Besides gathering to worship on Sundays, when we get together we tend to do two things: serve & enjoy life.

If you would like to contact Pastor Jim or our worship leader, Lee Heerspink, please click on their photo to email them directly.
